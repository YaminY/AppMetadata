Privacy Policy for Orint

Orint is designed around a local-first philosophy. Your habits, journals, reflections, and AI processing remain on your device by default.

We do not run analytics, advertising trackers, or backend systems that collect your personal habit or journal content.

Core features work without account creation or mandatory cloud infrastructure.

Optional permissions (such as microphone, calendar access, or notifications) are only used for features you explicitly enable and can be revoked at any time in your device settings.

AI models and application resources may be downloaded to your device to enable local features. Your personal journal or habit content is not uploaded for AI processing.

If you choose to export data, share logs, use platform sync services, or make purchases through app stores, those actions are handled through your selected platform or provider according to their own policies.

You remain in control of your local data, including deleting records, exporting backups, or removing all app data by uninstalling the application or clearing device storage.
